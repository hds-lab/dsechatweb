Connect and Communicate

The data science chat room is a social space where data scientists across NYU, Berkeley, and UW can gather:

  • Ask questions and share advice about data science.
  • Get to know other data scientists.
  • Socialize and build a data science community.

It's Science!

Group chat rooms are a great way for a geographically dispersed group of people to stay in touch. Since chat is less formal than email, it can be fun and social. At the same time, you can use the chat room to ask questions and find people who can help you solve work problems.

Read more about why chat is great for online collaboration.

Get started

Once you create an account, you can get started quickly using the web-based client, right in your browser. You can also connect to the chat room using any chat client that supports XMPP.

About us

The data science chat room is based at the University of Washington. It is operated by Professor Cecilia Aragon, Michael Brooks, and Brittany Fiore-Silfvast.

This project is part of a cross-campus collaborative data science effort supported by the Gordon and Betty Moore Foundation and the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation. Read more about this initiative.

For questions about the data science chat room, contact us. Learn more about Data Science at UW.

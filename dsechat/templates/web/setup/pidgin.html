Pidgin Configuration

Pidgin is a free IM program for Windows and Linux.

Download and install Pidgin. Then follow the steps below.

1. Add an account

If you have just installed Pidgin, you will see the Accounts screen (pictured).

You can also access this screen from Accounts > Manage Accounts.

Click Add... to setup your account.

2. Enter your account information

Set the Protocol to XMPP.

Enter your chat username.

For the Domain, enter {{ xmpp_server }}.

Enter your chat account password.

Click the Add button when done.

3. Enable your account

Make sure that small checkbox next to your account is checked.

This will make Pidgin sign in using your username and password.

You can close the Modify Accounts window.

4. Find the chat room

On the Buddy List window, under the Tools menu, choose Room List

5. Find the chat room

On the Room List window, click Get List.

The conference server name should be {{ xmpp_muc_server }}. Click Find Rooms.

Click on the {{ xmpp_muc_room }} room, and then click Add Chat.

You may use whatever Handle you wish, but we recommend you use your username.

You may leave the Password field empty.

Press the Add button.

6. Configure the room

Type your actual name for Handle. This is how others will see you in the chat room.

We suggest you check both boxes at the bottom, but the other fields can be ignored.

Press the Add button. Back on the Room List window (above), click Join.

You are now connected to the chat room.
You can find the room again in your Buddy List.
